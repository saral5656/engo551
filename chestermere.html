C H E S T E R M E R E

Quick Fact #1

Chestermere is located 10-15 minutes east of Calgary, Alberta.

Quick Fact #2

Officially became a city in 2015 but I still call it a town since it pales in comparison to any of the municipalities in the GTA.

Quick Fact #3

Main attraction is Chestermere Lake. It's fun to do outdoor activities in this lake during the summer like kayaking and swimming as long as you ignore the fact that the lake is filthy. The "beach" is basically the size of a sandbox.

Quick Fact #4

The baseball diamond at the local high school was built by a teacher and their detention students.

Quick Fact #5

It does not have a McDonalds or Walmart within its city limits. I strongly suspect that the reason they won't build these is that it will impose a monopoly on all the other restaurants and grocery stores in Chestermere.

Quick Fact #6

The former mayor, Jeff Colvin, was fired after spending more than $8500 on food and booze with the city credit card.
